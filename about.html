Hello :)

Hi, I am Robin Sola. Five years ago I moved to Portland, Oregon from Upstate NY to live with my partner, Stephanie and our Guatemalan rescue dog, Pepper. Before moving to the west coast, I was a graphic designer for a small town publishing company, mostly designing ads and logos for local businesses. Currently, I am an artisan bread baker for New Seasons Market. I work those early 4am shifts and get to personally hand over freshly baked bread to the SE Portland community. I am also a student at Epicodus, working my way back into the tech scene, advancing my skills in developing websites and applications.
