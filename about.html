Hello there! I'm Robin Sola.

In 2012 I moved to Portland, OR from upstate NY to live with my partner, Stephanie Adams-Santos, and our Guatemalan rescue dog. Before moving to the west coast, I was a graphic designer for a small town publishing company, mostly designing logos and ads for local businesses. Currently, I am an artisan bread baker at New Seasons Market as well as a student at Epicodus, working my way back into the tech scene. On my free time, I enjoy baking vegan pastries and bagels from scratch and exploring the beautiful Pacific Northwest.
